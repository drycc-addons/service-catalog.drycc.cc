Tasks

This section contains pages that show how to do individual tasks. A task page shows how to do a single thing, typically given by a short sequence of steps.

Install a Broker

You must register a broker with Service Catalog to make the services that broker offers available on your cluster.

Synchronize a Broker

When a broker’s catalog changes, it may need to be resynchronized with Service Catalog.

Remove a Stuck Instance or Binding

When a broker stops responding, a user may be unable to delete instances and bindings belonging to that broker. In such cases, the user may need to forcefully remove such ‘stuck’ instances or bindings.
